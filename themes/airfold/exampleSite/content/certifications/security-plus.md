## About This Certification

CompTIA Security+ is a global certification that validates the baseline skills necessary to perform core security functions.

## Skills Validated

- Threats, attacks, and vulnerabilities
- Technologies and tools
- Architecture and design
- Identity and access management
- Risk management
- Cryptography and PKI

## Why Security+?

Security+ is often required for government and defense positions, and is recognized worldwide as a fundamental cybersecurity certification.
